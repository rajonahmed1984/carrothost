FAQ

Common questions about BDIX VPS

What is BDIX and why does it matter?

BDIX (Bangladesh Internet Exchange) is a local internet exchange point where Bangladeshi ISPs peer directly. Traffic between BDIX-connected servers and local users never leaves the country, resulting in much lower latency and cost.

Can I upgrade my VPS resources later?

Yes. You can upgrade CPU, RAM, and storage anytime with just a reboot — no data migration needed. Downgrades are also available on request.

Which operating systems are available?

We offer Ubuntu, Debian, AlmaLinux, and CentOS out of the box. You can also upload your own ISO for custom operating systems.

Do you offer managed VPS services?

Our VPS plans are self-managed by default. We also offer a managed add-on where our team handles security patches, monitoring, and routine maintenance for you.
